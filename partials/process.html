How we work

A Clear Path From Assessment to Owned Systems.

You always know what phase we are in, who is involved, and what decisions are yours. We are practitioners building alongside your team—not a black-box agency that disappears into slides.

  1. Weeks 1–2

    Discovery & intake

    Stakeholder interviews, workflow mapping, and inventory of systems, data stores, and pain points. We align on success metrics before touching architecture.

  2. Weeks 2–4

    Readiness & gap analysis

    Score the five readiness pillars, document risks, and draft the use-case matrix. Optional technical spike on integrations (ERP exports, CRM, file shares).

  3. Weeks 4–6

    Roadmap & executive readout

    Prioritized roadmap, governance outline, and architecture sketch. You leave with artifacts you can execute internally or with us in a follow-on build.

  4. Build (optional)

    File architecture & agents

    Intelligent file layout, skill documents, and purpose-built agents wired to your workflows—local models where appropriate, frontier APIs only where they earn their cost.

  5. Ongoing

    Enablement & handoff

    Your team learns to maintain the structure, extend skills, and govern agents. We stay available for coaching—not perpetual dependency on rented SaaS.

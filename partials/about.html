About

A Small Team Building Serious Systems.

SaaS Squash is an industrial agentic consultancy based in Seattle. We help mid-market B2B organizations design intelligent file architectures, custom agents, and governance so AI does not become another rented black box. The work is collaborative, opinionated, and built to stay in your environment.

Our backgrounds

Our paths converge where the field meets the codebase. Jarrod spent more than a decade in industrial safety and supply at Mallory Safety and Supply, with earlier leadership in manufacturing at National Safety Apparel—working construction, manufacturing, and utility accounts across the Pacific Northwest and beyond. Alex and Devin spent years building and scaling platform engineering inside Procore (construction-tech SaaS): observability, reliability, telemetry standards, and the day-to-day reality of keeping thousands of engineers aligned.

That mix—distribution margins, channel programs, and on-the-ground selling plus how serious software is built and operated—is why we focus on systems you own: clear file structures, explicit governance, and agents scoped to how your business actually makes money—not generic chatbots layered on top of the same broken processes.

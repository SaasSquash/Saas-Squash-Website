The SaaS Squash System

Build a System, Not a Stack.

We don't sell you software. We walk alongside your team and help you build something that belongs entirely to you: an intelligent file architecture that makes AI dramatically more powerful while dramatically reducing its cost.

01

Intelligent File Architecture

A structured, maintained directory of markdown files that serves as the institutional memory of your business. Not a database. Not a CRM. A living document system that agents read like a briefing before every task — giving them the context they need without burning tokens on re-explanation.

02

Neural Context Networking

Files in the structure are not isolated documents — they reference each other, link to related context, and create an interconnected web of knowledge. An agent reading your customer file follows the thread to your pricing logic, then to your product substitution playbook, then to your rep coaching notes — all automatically.

03

Skill Documents & Custom Agents

Skill documents are markdown files that teach agents exactly how to complete specific tasks: draft proposals, analyze pricing, summarize accounts, coach reps, respond to RFPs. Agents are purpose-built workers that read those skills and execute defined jobs — creating a workforce of AI tools that know your business and follow your rules.

04

Local LLM Deployment

Not every task needs a frontier model. Summarizing meeting notes, drafting internal updates, classifying structured data — these workloads run efficiently on locally hosted language models. When the right context is pre-loaded by your file structure, even smaller local models perform at a high level. Zero token cost. Zero data leaving your environment.

The goal is a system where your agents arrive pre-briefed. They know your company. They know the task. They know the rules. The heavy lifting of context delivery has already been done — by the file structure, not by the API.
